# Remote Work Policy

## General Rules

Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week. Team agreements take priority over individual preferences. At least 2 days per week must be spent in the office. Wednesdays and Thursdays are preferred office days for most teams.

## Equipment Budget

Each employee receives a one-time EUR 500 budget for home office equipment. This covers: desk, chair, monitor, keyboard, mouse, headset. Submit receipts via the expense portal within 3 months of start date. The company provides a laptop and docking station.

## Availability Expectations

During core hours (10:00-15:00), you must be reachable via Teams. Keep your Teams status up to date. Respond to messages within 1 hour during working hours. Camera on during video calls is encouraged but not mandatory.

## Internet and Workspace

A stable internet connection is required (minimum 20 Mbps). You are responsible for a quiet, suitable workspace at home. VPN must be active when accessing internal systems. Do not work from public WiFi without VPN.

## Hybrid Meeting Guidelines

If any participant is remote, all participants join via Teams (even those in the office). Use the meeting room camera and microphone for group calls. Share your screen when presenting. Mute when not speaking.

## Health and Safety

Take regular breaks (at least 5 minutes every hour). Ensure proper desk and chair ergonomics. The company offers a free ergonomic assessment for your home office — request via HR portal. Report any work-related injuries, even at home.
