# Company Handbook

## Working Hours

Standard working hours are 09:00 to 17:30, Monday to Friday. Core hours (when everyone should be available) are 10:00 to 15:00. Flexible start between 07:30 and 10:00 is allowed with team agreement.

## Leave Policy

Every employee receives 25 leave days per year. Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance via the HR portal. Team leads approve leave requests. During holiday periods (July/August, Christmas), coordinate with your team to ensure coverage.

## Sick Leave

Report sick before 09:00 AM via the HR portal or by calling your team lead. No doctor's note needed for the first 2 days. From day 3 onward, a doctor's note is required. Full salary continues for the first 52 weeks of illness.

## Onboarding

New employees receive a laptop configured by IT on day one. Intune automatically installs required software within 30-60 minutes of first login. Additional tools can be installed from the Company Portal app. A buddy from your team will be assigned to guide you through the first month.

## Expense Policy

Business expenses are reimbursed via the expense portal. Receipts are required for all expenses over EUR 25. Travel expenses: public transport is preferred. Taxi only for trips after 22:00 or when public transport is unavailable. Meals during business travel: max EUR 30 per meal within the Netherlands.

## Dress Code

Business casual is the standard. No formal suit required. For client-facing meetings, dress one level above the client's dress code. Fridays are casual.

## Company Culture

We value transparency, collaboration, and continuous learning. Every team has a weekly standup and a bi-weekly retrospective. Monthly company-wide town halls are held on the last Friday of the month. Knowledge sharing sessions (tech talks) are every other Wednesday at 16:00.
