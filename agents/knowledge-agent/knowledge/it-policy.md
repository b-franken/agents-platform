# IT & Security Policy

## Password Requirements

Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character. Passwords expire every 90 days. Do not reuse your last 10 passwords. Use Microsoft Authenticator for MFA on all company accounts.

## VPN Access

Use Cisco AnyConnect to connect to the company VPN. Primary server: vpn.company.nl. Backup server: vpn2.company.nl. VPN is required for accessing internal systems from outside the office. Split tunneling is enabled — only company traffic goes through the VPN.

## BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

Personal devices may be used for email and Teams only if enrolled in Intune. Personal devices must have a screen lock and encryption enabled. Company data on personal devices can be remotely wiped if the device is lost or stolen.

## Data Classification

All data must be classified as: Public, Internal, Confidential, or Strictly Confidential. Default classification is Internal. Confidential and Strictly Confidential data must not be stored on personal devices or shared via unapproved channels. Use Azure Information Protection labels in Office documents.

## Acceptable Use

Company equipment is for business use. Limited personal use is acceptable. Do not install unauthorized software. Report any security incidents immediately to security@company.nl. Phishing emails should be reported via the Outlook "Report Phishing" button.

## Backup and Recovery

All company data should be stored in OneDrive or SharePoint (automatically backed up). Local hard drive data is not backed up. Recovery time objective (RTO): 4 hours for critical systems, 24 hours for non-critical.

## Software Requests

Standard software is available in the Company Portal app. For non-standard software, submit a request via the IT portal with business justification. Approval typically takes 2-3 business days. Open-source software must be approved by the security team.
